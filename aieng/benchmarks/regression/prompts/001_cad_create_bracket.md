Create an aluminum L-bracket. The vertical leg is 60mm tall, 40mm wide, and 5mm thick.
The horizontal leg is 50mm long, 40mm wide, and 5mm thick. Add an 8mm diameter
mounting hole centered 15mm from the top of the vertical leg.
Name the part "bracket" and color it aluminum-grey.
